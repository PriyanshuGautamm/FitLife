Heart Health & Awareness

About Human Heart

The human heart is a powerful muscular organ that pumps blood throughout the body. It continuously supplies oxygen and nutrients while removing carbon dioxide and waste. A healthy heart ensures the proper functioning of every organ. Modern lifestyles, stress, and poor diets often affect heart health, making awareness and prevention essential.

Heart Attack vs Cardiac Arrest

Heart Attack

A heart attack occurs when blood flow to part of the heart is blocked, often by a blood clot. It damages heart muscles and requires immediate medical care.

Cardiac Arrest

Cardiac arrest happens when the heart suddenly stops beating. It is more serious than a heart attack and requires CPR or defibrillation immediately.

Symptoms of Heart Disease

  • Chest pain or pressure
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dizziness or fainting
  • Irregular heartbeat
  • Fatigue or weakness

Precautions for Heart Health

  • Eat a balanced, low-fat diet
  • Exercise regularly (30 min daily walk)
  • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol
  • Manage stress and get enough sleep
  • Go for regular heart check-ups
