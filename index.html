Quick Information

What is BMI?

BMI or Body Mass Index is a simple calculation that uses a person's height and weight to estimate body fat and categorize them into group like Underweight, Normal, Overweight and Obese.

How to calculate BMI?

The standard formula of BMI: It is the ratio of person's body weight (in kg) and the square of person's height (in m).

BMI = Weight (kg)/ (Height (m))^2

BMI Table

Underweight: BMI below 18.5

Normal: BMI 18.5-24.9

Overweight: BMI>25.0

Obese: BMI>30.0

Know Your Heart

A healthy heart beats regularly, pumps blood efficiently, and keeps you energetic. Learn how to check your heart health.

Common diseases: Coronary artery disease, Heart attack, Arrhythmia, Hypertension.

Symptoms: Chest pain, shortness of breath, dizziness, fatigue.

Precautionary measures: Balanced diet, regular exercise, avoid smoking, manage stress.
